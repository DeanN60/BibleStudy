Dispensationalism is a study of the dispensations found in the Bible. It is a process where we rightly divide the Word of Truth.

Premillennialism (or futurism) is usually dispensational in their belief. They look forward to the Millennium Reign of Christ as spoken of in the Bible.

John Nelson Darby is often considered the one who created the idea of Dispensationalism. In reality dispensationalism was discussed long before Darby was born. The idea of dispensationalism was taught as early as 150AD.

is a futurist premillennialism view.

in 150AD Justin Martyr supported the idea that Jerusalem would be rebuilt.

in 170AD Irenaeus claimed that the heavenly Jerusalem would come down some day and that Christ would physically rein here on earth.

Today, there are many people attacking dispensationalism. Most of these people do not understand what dispensationalism is. Dispensationalism is a way of ordering phases of world history based upon what we observe in scripture.
