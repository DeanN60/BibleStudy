We need salvation because we are sinners. Not because of what we have done, but because we are fallen.

says that we all have sinned and fallen short of the glory of God.

We are fallen because we inherited our sin nature. It is in our DNA you might say. It is not because of something we did. Therefore there is nothing we can do to erase it.

It is a no-win situation. Because we are fallen, we have no hope from our own effort. We cannot win because we have a fallen nature. Without Jesus' death on the cross, we cannot be saved from sin.

What does a fallen nature mean? It means that we are separated from God and unable to do anything to get back to Him. This is not my words, but God's words.

God had pity upon us and provided a way out of this situation.

explains that we can only go through Jesus to get to the Father.

talks about how His stripes heal us. Nothing that we can do.
