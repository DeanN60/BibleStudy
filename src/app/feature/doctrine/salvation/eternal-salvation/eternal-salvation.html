Lost Opportunity

talks about those who have been enlightened with the Gospel are unable to repent and come back to Jesus. But wait! can we lose our salvation?

What is talking about is a person who was exposed to the plan of salvation, but never took it serious. This person has rejected Jesus even after hearing the gospel and the Holy Spirit speaking to them. In this verse it says that they were enlightened which means exposed to the Gospel message. Having tasted His good word, meaning that they were exposed to the truth of Jesus.

We Are Saved But Still Sin

says that we are still sinners and must confess our sin. He is faithful to forgive us our sins. If we say we have no sin, then the truth is not in us and we make God a liar.

Our New Incorruptible Body

talks about the day when we put off this corruptible body and put on a new Incorruptible body. This is when we will be made perfect and sinless. This is the resurrection.

Summary

If we truly believe and have accepted Jesus's death of the cross as payment for our sin, then we are saved. We are sealed by the Holy Spirit and can not lose our salvation.
