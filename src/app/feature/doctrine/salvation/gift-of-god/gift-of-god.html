says that no one can access the Father except through Jesus. This means that no work or any other religious belief will get you into Heaven. Note that this means that if you are trusting in anything else to get you into Heaven, you will not make it.

says that the wages of sin is death but the gift of God is eternal life. This means that the payment for our existing state is death. Death means that we are separated from God, which is we are sent to the lake of eternal fire.

Default State

We are sinners by default. Not because of anything that we have done. But because our first parents sinned against God in the garden of Eden.

This is not fair, but God has provided a way for us to be saved from the lake of eternal fire, by accepting His son Jesus as our personal Savoir.

says that God does not want anyone to perish in the lake of eternal fire. He wants us all to repent.

God doesn't force anyone to accept Jesus as payment for their debt of sin. He wants us to want Him and Jesus. This is more than just saying that we want Him and Jesus.
