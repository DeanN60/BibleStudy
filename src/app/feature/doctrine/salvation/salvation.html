Salvation

Salvation is by grace alone and not of any works. Salvation is a gift of God freely given to all who receive it.
Ephesians 2:8-10
8 For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith-and this is not form yourselves, it is the gift of God- 9 not by works, so that no one can boast. 10 For we are God's handiwork, created in Christ Jesus to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do.
NIV, Holy Bible (p. 3211). Zondervan. Kindle Edition.
Salvation is a gift from God. It can not be earned as seen in Ephesians 2:8-10
