Salvation is by grace alone and not of any works. Salvation is a gift of God freely given to all who receive it.

says that everyone has sinned. This is not talking about what they have done wrong, but their legal state before God.

says that no one is righteous. We are all cursed.

says that the wages of sin is death. We all deserve death.

says that God has mercy upon us. That He gave His only son for us so we might be reconciled to Him.

says that if we confess with our mouth that Jesus is our Lord and saviour, then we will be saved.

says that anyone who calls upon the name of the Lord will be saved. This means that we must humble ourselves and accept Jesus's death on the cross as payment for our sin debt.

says that we have peace with God because the debt of sin has been paid by Jesus Christ.

says that there is no longer any condemnation for those who accept the death of Jesus on the cross as payment for our debt of sin.

says that nothing can separate us from the love of God.

says that salvation is a free gift from God that only Jesus can provide.

says that since it is of grace that we are saved. No works of our own can accomplish this salvation.

say that this salvation is not something that we have accomplished. Salvation is not something that is due to us. Salvation is a gift of God.
