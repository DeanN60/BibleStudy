Eschatology is the study of End Times prophecies.

Many people assume that Revelation is the only book on Eschatology. Actually many books in the Bible contain End Time prophecies. To understand the prophecies in Revelation, you must be familiar with many Old Testament passages.

Not all prophecies deal with End Times.

All prophecies before End Times prophecies all happened literally. Because of this it is expected that all future prophecies will also be literal.

Many people believe that future prophecies will be allegorical in nature. Part of the reason for this is many prophecies contain language which is very strange and hard to imagine.

You may not properly understand End Time prophecies if you believe that the Church and Israel are the same.
