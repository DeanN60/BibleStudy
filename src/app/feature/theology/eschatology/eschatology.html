Eschatology is the study of End Times prophecies.

Many people assume that Revelation is the only book on Eschatology. Actually many books in the Bible contain End Time prophecies. To understand the prophecies in Revelation, you must be familiar with many Old Testament passages. Note that not all prophecies deal with End Times.

All prophecies before End Times prophecies all happened literally. Because of this it is expected that all future prophecies will also occur literally.

Many people believe that future prophecies will be allegorical in nature. Part of the reason for this is many prophecies contain language which is very strange and hard to imagine. This is understandable since some of these prophecies are bizarre and seem far fetched.

You may not properly understand End Time prophecies if you believe that the Church and Israel are the same. An understanding of the purpose of Israel and the purpose of the Church are critical to understanding future prophecies.

Israel brought us the Old Testament and the Law. Through Israel we have Jesus the Messiah.

The Church started out as only Jewish believers, but later gentiles believed as well. The Church was commissioned to go into all the world and teach the Gospel.

There are several views on End Times prophecies. The view you hold to does not determine your salvation. As long as you are trusting Jesus and nothing else, you are saved.
