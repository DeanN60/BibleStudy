says that when the Son of Man comes in His glory, He will separate the sheep from the goats. It is important to note here that the time mentioned here is when He comes in His Glory. This is His second coming. The sheep being the ones who follow Him and the goats being the ones who do not. He shall place the sheep on His right side and the goats on His left side.

says that the King will say to those on His right that they are blessed and that they will inherit the kingdom.

says that the King will say to those on His left that they are accursed and are cast into the eternal fire which was prepared for the devil and his angels.

Note that the sheep are not taken away, but are kept here on the earth to enter into the kingdom. Where the goats are taken and cast into the eternal fire.
