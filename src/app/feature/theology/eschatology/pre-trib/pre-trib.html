In Irenaeus speaks about the Church being raptured before the tribulation.

In we are told that Jesus is going to Heaven to prepare a place for us. Jesus said that he will come back for us so that where He is we should be with Him.

Compare with where it says that the earth will give up its dead. They are told to go into their chambers until the indignation is past. It then goes on to explain what the indignation is. It is the wrath of God upon the earth.

Issues

Some say that the sheep spoken of in is the rapture. Take note that the sheep do not receive an incorruptible body. They are kept here on the earth and not taken to heaven. See Sheep and Goats. This is not an issue.
